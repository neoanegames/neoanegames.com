Forastero

Coming soon to Xbox Series S|X and PlayStation 5

Story

In a land plagued by greed and violence, a government mercenary is hired to recover a mysterious jewel with a hidden power. Driven by the promise of wealth, he embarks on a mission that quickly spirals out of control.

During his journey, a native warrior seeking revenge and a corrupt banker with hidden agendas cross his path, complicating the mission.

However, the improper possession of the jewel could result in the destruction of the world, revealing that greed carries the weight to change the course of humanity.

Gameplay

  • Intense action with strategic depth in melee combat.
  • Agile dodges and counterattacks for frantic duels.
  • Short and long-range weapons with unique styles.
  • An Ultimate meter that accumulates ammo to unleash devastating attacks.
  • Special techniques that allow aerial combos and enemy defense breaking.

Features

  • Upgrade shop to unlock new moves, special abilities, and improve attributes.
  • Adaptive AI: enemies learn from the player, studying their behavior and making each battle unique and challenging.
  • Stylized visuals inspired by Wild West comic books.
  • Finisher system and Quick Time Events for cinematic and brutal moments.
  • Varied enemies with unique reactions, doses of dark humor, and striking cultural references.

System Requirements

Minimum:

  • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6500 / Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 750 Ti / AMD Radeon RX 460
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Sound Card: DirectX Compatible

Recommended:

  • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
  • OS: Windows 10 / 11
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-6700 / Ryzen 5 1500X
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 / AMD Radeon RX 560
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Sound Card: DirectX Compatible
